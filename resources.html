Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell appliances or parts?

No. We install appliances customers already purchased, and we install parts only for repair jobs assigned to us.

Can I get a quote before you arrive?

We can offer a rough estimate based on symptoms, but an in-person assessment is required for an accurate quote.

Do you charge by the hour?

No. We charge by the job, not by the hour.

Do you offer a service guarantee?

Yes. Completed repairs are covered by a 90-day warranty on parts and labor.
